Click To add Note

You can see an animated button. Click the animated button to create note,You can add title and description. You can find 4 buttons:

  • Save
  • Reminder
  • Favourite
  • Cancel

Notes Page

In this page, you can view all your saved notes. This notes helps you in memorizing daily activities or the important information.

Reminder Page

You can use this option inorder to get reminders at the time you wanted to do your task, In this reminder page you can see all the notes which are to be reminded.

Favourite Page

All the notes which makes you happy or something special can be added to this Favourite page.

You can also unfavourite your notes in this page.

Archive Page

The notes which are not necessary to appear on the notes page can be archived. you can find the archived content in this archive page

Notes can be unarchived whenever you required.

Trash Page

All the deleted notes appears in the trash page

In this trash you have two operations

1.Restore

You can restore your note whenever it is required.

2.Delete

If you want to delete the notes permenantly you can delete it .

SignOut

You can logout whenever you want

Note:

The session gets expired after certain time so dont get worried. If you relogin you can view all your notes and further continue with your work.
